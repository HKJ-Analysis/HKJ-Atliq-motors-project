🚗 Electric Vehicle Market Analysis - AtliQ Motors

📊 Codebasics Resume Project Challenge 12

Welcome to the Electric Vehicle (EV) Market Analysis project repository! This project is a part of the Codebasics Resume Project Challenge 12, where I had the opportunity to analyze the Indian EV market for AtliQ Motors, a leading American automotive company expanding into the electric vehicle sector in India.

🚀 Project Overview

Problem Statement: AtliQ Motors, a major American automotive manufacturer, has successfully established itself globally, particularly in the electric vehicle sector. With plans to expand into the Indian market, the company aims to launch both 2-wheelers and 4-wheelers EVs in India. However, to ensure successful entry, a detailed analysis of the Indian EV market is necessary.

Objective: The goal of this project is to provide AtliQ Motors with actionable insights on the Indian EV market. This includes identifying the best states for setting up manufacturing plants, understanding customer adoption rates, exploring government schemes, and formulating strategies to help AtliQ Motors effectively operate and compete in the Indian EV market.

🔧 Tool Stack

Visualization Tool & Analysis : Power BI

🌟 Key Insights

Revenue Growth: The profit from 2022 to 2024 shows a higher growth rate compared to 2023 to 2024, primarily due to the FAME II subsidy scheme.
Market Leader: Tata Motors leads the 4-wheeler market consistently across all quarters.
CAGR Analysis: BYD India is outperforming other manufacturers in CAGR, indicating strong growth potential.
Sales Trends: 2-wheelers dominate the EV market, accounting for 92.6% of total EV sales, with March being the peak sales month due to fiscal year-end discounts and tax benefits.

💡 Recommendations

Prioritize two-wheeler EVs and utilize government incentives: Capitalize on the growing demand for affordable electric two-wheelers in India and leverage subsidies and tax benefits to enhance market competitiveness.
Invest in cutting-edge battery technology: Focus on research and development to improve battery efficiency, lifetime, and cost-effectiveness, ensuring AtliQ Motors stays ahead of the competition.
Expand charging infrastructure and offer comprehensive services: Partner with local entities to increase the availability of charging stations and provide exceptional after-sales support to enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty.
